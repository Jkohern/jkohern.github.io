Data Encoding Table

Playing Inside: Every time someone started playing a game, the time they started playing, the game they
started playing, etc. is encoded. By combining all of the components, it is possible to find out when people
played and what they played.
COMPONENT TYPE NUMBER OF BITS/RANGE DESCRIPTION/COMMENTS
Who Played ASCII 256 bits (0-32 chars) Name of the person playing
What they played ASCII 800 bits (0-100 chars) The game that the person played
Device played on ASCII 256 bits (0-32 chars) Name of what they were playing on
How long they played Number 9 bits (0-511 mins) (0-8.5 hrs) The amount of time they played
Order they started playing Number 7 bits (0-127 chars) The order of when they started playing
